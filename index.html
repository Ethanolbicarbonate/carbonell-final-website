About His Art

His Thing About Art

Ethan’s old-school style involves getting down with ink and graphite. Picture him hunched over a desk, pen in hand, creating lines stroke by stroke. It’s like he’s whispering secrets to the paper. Now, Ethan’s digital playground is where the magic happens. Imagine him with a stylus, conjuring vibrant colors and cute little scenes on a screen. The best part? He can hit “undo” if things go wonky. Studio Ghibli vibes, anyone? His art isn’t about realism; it’s about feels. You know, those moments when you catch a firefly or smell fresh rain. Yeah, that’s what he’s after.

Commissions and Cool Stuff

Ethan’s not just a procrastinator; he’s a multitasker. Commissions? Yep, he’ll turn your ideas into something you'd like to see. But wait, there’s more! He designs logos, slaps his art on T-shirts, mugs, prints, stickers—you name it. So, if you want a dash of wonder in your life, check out his stuff in this website. Though, theres not much to see since hes been busy with school stuff recently.
